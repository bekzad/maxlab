eNotify Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2nd 2020

Definitions:

  • Any use of (us/we/I) refers to YOONKA LIMITED

  • eNotify refers to the full paid version “eNotify” and the free version “eNotify Lite”

  • Message refers to an Email/SMS/MMS or other type of text message

  • Information refers to any information including private information

YOONKA LIMITED distributes eNotify via GooglePlay and formerly via Amazon under the package names com.hermes.enotify and com.hermes.enotifylite. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of personal information we receive from users of eNotify.

We use your personal information only for providing the functionality of eNotify. By using eNotify, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We only distribute eNotify via GooglePlay and formerly via Amazon. Other entities distributing eNotify are doing so without the consent of YOONKA LIMITED.

The primary purpose of eNotify is to reveal email and text messages on a mobile device. The user should avoid using eNotify in public locations as the notifications may reveal private message content.

Information Collection And Use

eNotify is software, that allows users to integrate and manage their online communications, including email and other types of text messaging. Each eNotify user chooses which online accounts he or she wishes to access using eNotify.

When you set up eNotify to access your email account, you will need to enter information and credentials for that account, such as your name, email address, and password. eNotify stores this information on your mobile device.

In addition to your credentials, eNotify needs certain Non-Personal, technical information from your account providers to configure each email account you use. eNotify stores other information on your mobile device, including copies of your email messages. eNotify stores this information locally on your mobile device and does not transmit it except to accessories such as smartwatches and other connected devices.

eNotify attempts to make a direct local connection from your mobile device to your remote mail server. The processing of mail retrieved from your remote mail server is performed locally on the device. The user must ensure that the network, mail server and device are secure.

eNotify stores private information in a local database. The information is required to implement features including but not limited to: notifications, commands, message viewing & history. The information includes, but is not limited to: senders, recipients, subjects and message contents. Information judged to be private is usually encrypted by us however it is not guaranteed to be encrypted. Furthermore, we do not guarantee the quality or reliability of any encryption that we use within the app. It is the user's responsibility to encrypt their Android device. The Android device can typically be encrypted from the device's security settings or with 3rd party software. Furthermore, it is the user's responsibility to ensure that all data whether encrypted or not is kept locally and that the device is kept in their possession.

Log Data

eNotify collects logging data on the device to allow users to diagnose problems with the app. The logs may be stored both in memory and/or on the SD card depending on app options. Message details may be contained in the logs.

We do not typically store logging data on our servers, however you may send logs to YOONKA LIMITED by selecting the email option on the 'View Logs' page. It is the user's responsibility to ensure that the logs sent to us do not contain any private information. Logs that you send to us may be stored on our servers to help improve eNotify in the future.

The user must ensure that logs are removed when no longer needed. Additionally, logs may be stored on the SD card. The user is responsible for encrypting the SD card. On most Android devices, the SD card can by encrypted from the device's security settings or with 3rd party software.

Features that Reveal Information

eNotify reveals message details in the following features:

Android framework notifications, Popup notifications, Smartwatches and accessory notifications, Simple message viewer, Home screen widget.

Enotify is capable of reading text to speech alerts. Text to speech alerts may directly reveal private information to any entity within hearing distance of your device.

eNotify is capable of displaying sensitive information on your lock screen.

eNotify may have other features that reveal sensitive or private information.

eNotify is capable of recording custom alert sounds.

Consequences

The loss of privacy resulting from eNotify alerts could have unwanted social consequences including but not limited to: Loss of employment or a financial opportunity, loss of relationships or marriage, personal embarrassment or humiliation, loss of reputation, arrest and incarceration.

Additionally, do not use eNotify if you have a heart condition or other medical condition that could be aggravated by unexpected alert sounds.

We do not use information that you enter into the app to contact you. The information is stored locally on your device so we normally do not have any way to initiate contact with you.

Security

The security of your information is important to us, but no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is absolutely secure.

While we attempt to protect your information we cannot guarantee it will be protected.

The user is encouraged to encrypt their internal Android device and SD card using the security settings available on their Android device or via 3rd party software.

The purpose of eNotify is to reveal email and SMS information via notifications. As such, the app will inevitably reveal private information. Therefore users should assess for themselves whether they want to use eNotify.
